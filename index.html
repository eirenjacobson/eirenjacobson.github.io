Eiren Kate Jacobson

About Me

I am interested in quantitative approaches to cetacean population studies. I received my Ph.D. in Oceanography from the Scripps Institution of Oceanography in 2017. Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Washington School of Aquatic and Fishery Sciences and a Research Fellow at the Centre for Research into Ecological and Environmental Modelling at the University of St Andrews.

I live in Scotland, where I enjoy hiking, reading, quilting, and spending time with my dog Juniper, cat Fern, and partner Dave.
